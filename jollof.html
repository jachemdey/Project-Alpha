Jollof Rice
Ingredients:
6 large red bell peppers
6 medium sized tomatoes
2 large onions
3 habanero peppers
1 bulb garlic
2 teaspoons curry powder
3/4 cup cooking oil
2 bay leaves
4 cups basmati rice
1.5 cups pan drippings (from previously baked chicken)
2.5 cups water
salt to taste.
String beans Ingredients:
About 2 Lb green beans (string beans)
4-5 cloves garlic, sliced
salt and pepper to taste
Enjoy!
